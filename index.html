The Protocol

By Producers, For Producers

Solar Media is a premier electronic label dedicated to the pure, unadulterated frequencies of the underground. Specializing heavily in Brazilian Funk and Phonk, we exist to amplify artists who break the mold and refuse to conform to algorithmic standards.

Founded by producers, for producers. We understand the architecture of the independent scene because we live it. We know what it takes to cut through the static, and our mission is to provide a platform that elevates authentic, boundary-pushing music.

We aren't just releasing tracks; we are engineering a movement. If your sound hits hard and shifts the culture, this is your home.
